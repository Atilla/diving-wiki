# Megafauna Encounter Matrix

Where to reliably encounter key marine megafauna, based on current data across all 66 destinations. Reliability reflects post-bleaching conditions and recent trip reports, not historical marketing.

**Reliability scale:**
- **Near-guaranteed** — >90% encounter rate in season
- **Reliable** — regular sightings most dives in season
- **Seasonal** — present in a defined window but not every dive
- **Declining** — historically reliable but recent data shows significant drops

See [Strategic Takeaway #9](strategic-takeaways.md) on outdated megafauna marketing.
