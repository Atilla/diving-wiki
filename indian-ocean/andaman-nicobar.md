# Andaman & Nicobar Islands

## Status

**GOOD — India's premier dive destination; Barren Island volcano dive is unique**

- Located in the Bay of Bengal/Andaman Sea, the Andamans offer the most accessible "Southeast Asian-quality" reef diving under the Indian flag. **Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep)** is the main dive hub. Good tropical reef diving with healthy hard corals, decent diversity, regular manta ray sightings, and 15–35m visibility.
- **Barren Island** is India's only active volcano and offers genuinely unique diving — black volcanic sand slopes, thermal vents, and manta ray cleaning stations. Requires a 4–6 hour boat trip from Havelock or liveaboard access. Permission from Indian authorities can be intermittent.

## Key Dive Sites

#### The Wall, Lighthouse, Dixon's Pinnacle, Johnny's Gorge (Havelock area)

Established reef sites with good coral, reef fish diversity, turtles, manta rays.

#### Barren Island

Volcanic topography, black sand, manta cleaning stations. Advanced — weather-dependent, long transit.

## Marine Life

- **Manta rays**: Regular sightings at Havelock sites and Barren Island cleaning stations.
- **Turtles**: Common at reef sites.
- **Reef fish**: Good diversity for Indian Ocean.

## Dive Conditions

- **Temperature:** 26–29C Oct–May. Slightly cooler during monsoon transition months. 3mm sufficient.
- **Visibility**: 15-35m.
- **Currents**: Variable.

## Season

- **Best**: October–May. Monsoon (Jun–Sep) significantly limits diving — rough seas, poor visibility, many operators close.

## Practical Info

- **Access**: Foreigners can visit the Andaman Islands (Nicobar Islands remain off-limits). Restricted Area Permit issued on arrival at Port Blair airport — largely a formality. Fly from Chennai, Kolkata, or Delhi via connections.
- **Operators**: Andaman Bubbles, Dive India, Barefoot Scuba, Lacadives (all Havelock). MV Infiniti (liveaboard — covers Barren Island and outer sites). Infinity Scuba offers Barren Island trips.
- **Costs**: Very affordable — diving ~INR 4,000–7,000/dive ($50–85 USD). Accommodation from budget ($15–30/night) to mid-range ($80–200/night).
- **Recompression chamber**: Port Blair has a chamber. DAN insurance recommended for Barren Island trips.
- **Note**: Tourism growth (particularly domestic Indian) is increasing pressure on Havelock's reefs.
